# V3 Employer Review

## Recruiter
The platform separates candidate retrieval, relevance classification and ranking and exposes bounded lexical, semantic and hybrid search. The result is credible because standalone semantic underperformance and hybrid uncertainty remain visible.

## Technical hiring manager
Catalogue contracts, Turkish-aware normalization, sparse TF-IDF, explicit BM25, pinned multilingual E5, normalized NumPy indexing, validation-only RRF selection, five group-safe seeds, Recall@K, query bootstrap, latency/index cost, fingerprints and graceful missing-asset behavior are inspectable.

## Product stakeholder
Retrieval discovers candidates; V1 scores query-product relevance; ranking orders candidates. The bounded scope, incomplete judgments and absence of business-impact measurement are explicit.

## Search engineering
Top-K candidate generation, sparse and dense scoring, prefixes, BM25 and RRF parameters, candidate Recall@50 and segment-level help/harm are documented. RRF improves mean Recall@50 while one seed and its confidence interval keep the governance decision conservative.
